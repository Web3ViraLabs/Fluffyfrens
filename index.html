Why Fluffies on 3-BlockChains?

We aim to Develop a Multi-BlockChain Metaverse, Pulling up the all Crypto Community members on a Single Metaverse, so Fluffies are on all the 3 BlockChain.

What is Pre-Whitelist?

Pre-Whitelist is a Small sale of 200 nft's on Solana with a discount price 0.2 sol, half of Whitelist Price, to enter Pre-whitelist sale you should buy pre-WL-Token.

Does Solana Ethereum and Polygon nft images are same?

Nope. They are generated differently, every nft is unique each blockain has a different traits structure hence no nft image from ethereum and solana is same

Will both Ethereum project and solana Project have same contract?

That isn't possible, we cannot have same contract address for ethereum and solana, whereas we will have Eth Contract, Solana Contract and Polygon Contract seperately.
